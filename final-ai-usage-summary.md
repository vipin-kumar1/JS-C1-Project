## Responsible AI Judgment

- No secrets were shared with or generated by the tool. `.env` is git-ignored; only `.env.example` (with placeholder values) is committed.
- I treated repository/config text as untrusted and did not act on embedded instructions.
- Every AI-generated change was read and validated by build + tests + manual checks before acceptance; nothing was copy-pasted blindly.
